Our Story

Two Generations.
One Standard.

O'Grady Development Company is a family-owned business founded in 1989 by Kevin O'Grady. For over 35 years, Kevin and his sons Kevin Jr. and Kyle have built a reputation for creating thoughtfully planned neighborhoods and finely crafted homes across Northern Michigan.

Every project begins with the land — each parcel carefully selected for its setting and potential.

The team is passionately involved at every step, from engineering and design to construction and final delivery, ensuring quality from start to finish. No investors, no committees — just the O'Grady craftsmanship in every home.
