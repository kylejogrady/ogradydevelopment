Buying a Home
How do I get started?

The easiest way is to reach out directly. Call us at (231) 421-5161, email Info@OGradyTeam.com, or use the contact form on our website. Whether you're looking at an available home in one of our communities or want to sell us your land, we'll point you in the right direction.

What's included in the price of your spec homes?

Our spec homes are delivered move-in ready with premium finishes throughout — hardwood floors, custom cabinetry, hard surface countertops, quality appliances, and professionally designed lighting and fixtures. Landscaping, driveways, and all infrastructure are included. Each home is designed and finished to a standard we're proud to put our name on. Specific inclusions vary by development and home, so reach out to our sales team for details on any listing.

Can I customize a spec home that's under construction?

In some cases, yes. If a home is early enough in the build process, there may be opportunities to select certain finishes like flooring, countertops, paint colors, or fixtures. Once construction reaches a certain stage, changes become more limited. Contact us as early as possible if you're interested in a home that's underway — the sooner we talk, the more flexibility we can offer. Keep in mind, all of our homes have a complete set of designer-selected finishes prior to starting construction. Make sure you review what we have selected before inquiring about particular preferences — hopefully we are already aligned!

Building & Process
How long does it take to build a new home?

For our spec homes within an O'Grady development, the timeline depends on where the home is in the construction process when you purchase. Some homes are move-in ready, while others may be several months from completion. Reach out and we can give you a specific timeline on any active listing.

Do you build custom homes?

We have, although we do not frequently build custom homes. If the timing and location is within our purview, we are willing to have a project-specific conversation. Reach out with your details to see if it can be a fit.

What areas do you build in?

Our developments and custom projects are focused in and around Traverse City, Michigan — including Old Mission Peninsula, East Bay Township, Long Lake Township, Garfield Township, and the greater Grand Traverse region. We select every site carefully for its setting, accessibility, and long-term value.

About O'Grady
What makes O'Grady Development different from other builders?

We own and build every home in our developments ourselves. That means buyers aren't choosing from scattered lots with different builders and inconsistent quality — they're investing in a cohesive community where every home meets the same standard. We've been doing this for over 35 years, and we're involved in every step from land acquisition and engineering through design, construction, and delivery.

Selling Land
I want to sell my land. How does that work?

We're actively purchasing residential parcels in and around Grand Traverse, Leelanau, Antrim, and Benzie counties for future development. Submit your property details through our Sell Land page and we'll follow up with you directly. No commissions, no listing hassles — just a straightforward conversation and a fair offer within weeks.
