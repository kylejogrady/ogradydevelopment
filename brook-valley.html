Brook Valley is O'Grady Development's newest subdivision in Long Lake Township — just 4 miles west of downtown Traverse City. Each lot averages over half an acre with wooded backyards that offer natural privacy and a genuine sense of space, all on a brand new road with fresh utilities already in the ground.
