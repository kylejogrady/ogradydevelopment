Our Story

Two
Generations.
One Standard.

O'Grady Development Company is a family-owned business founded in 1989 by Kevin O'Grady. For over 35 years, Kevin and his son Kyle have built a reputation for creating thoughtfully planned neighborhoods and finely crafted homes.

Our Approach

Built From
the Ground Up.

Every O'Grady project begins with the land. Each parcel is carefully selected for its setting and potential, then developed with creative site layouts, efficient infrastructure, and homes designed to feel perfectly in place.

The team remains involved at every step — from engineering and design to construction and final delivery.

O'Grady Development is known for premium spec homes that reflect precision, practicality, and timeless design. Each home is built for its specific location, delivering a move-in-ready product that exceeds expectations and holds long-term value.

Our Promise

Build Something That Stands the Test of Time.

At O'Grady Development, passion meets purpose. Whether developing a new community or crafting a one-of-a-kind home, the standard is the same — enduring places to live, grow, and gather.
