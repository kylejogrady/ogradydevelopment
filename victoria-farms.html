About the Community

Where Nature
Meets Design

Victoria Farms is a brand new residential subdivision located off Three Mile Road in East Bay Township, approximately 20 minutes south of downtown Traverse City. The community sits on scenic rolling terrain surrounded by farmland, orchards, and natural beauty.

Every home in Victoria Farms is fully designed and built by O'Grady Development — no outside builders, no inconsistent quality. Each home is thoughtfully planned for its specific lot, with premium finishes, efficient floor plans, and timeless curb appeal.

The community offers a mix of single-family home designs ranging from 2,400 to 3,800+ square feet, with 3 to 5 bedrooms. Homes feature open-concept living, main-floor primary suites, hardwood flooring, custom cabinetry, stone countertops, and high-end appliances.
